    Digital marketing is the practice of promoting products, services, or brands through digital channels to reach and engage with a target audience. It encompasses a wide range of tactics and strategies, including search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, pay-per-click advertising (PPC), and mobile marketing. Digital marketing leverages various digital platforms and tools to connect with customers where they spend a significant amount of their time: online. Its effectiveness can be measured with precision through analytics, allowing for the optimization of campaigns and strategies in real time. The goal of digital marketing is to increase brand awareness, drive traffic, generate leads, and convert those leads into customers.
